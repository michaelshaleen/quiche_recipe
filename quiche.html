The Shaleen Swirl

Prehistoric recipies mixed with American fusion brings us to today's meal

Ingredients

  • Two chicken eggs
  • Four ounces Mozarella cheese
  • Chopped onions
  • Chopped bell peppers
  • Croissant

Instructions

  1. Mix prepped ingredients in bowl
  2. Pour mix into croissant
  3. Bake at 350 Fahrenheit
  4. Bake for 12minutes
  5. Let set and cool for 5minutes
